About

Nicholas MacGregor Garcia is a Lecturer in Information Systems at the National University of Singapore and Deputy Director of the NUS FinTech Lab, where he leads industry relations. He teaches financial technology, AI governance, and blockchain, and holds a PhD in Information Systems from NYU Stern.

His research looks at how organizations actually govern and deploy AI and financial systems — from agent-based simulations of payment infrastructure to the question of who answers for a capable system when it gets something wrong. Most of the material here is live rather than finished. If something is useful or off, find me on GitHub.
